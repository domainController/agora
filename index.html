Pre-Register for the OB1 Forum

This is a pre-registration form to help us better understand who you are and what you need. You’ll receive your access credentials once the platform opens.

OB1 stands for Obekväm arbetstid in Swedish — or “inconvenient working hours” in English. It refers to jobs with unstable schedules, unpredictable shifts, and working conditions that often go unseen or unspoken. This space was created in the winter of 2024, in Malmö, Sweden, by a dishwasher who believed that no one should face abuse alone. Whether you've been mistreated, silenced, or isolated — OB1 is here to help you be heard, and to find others who’ve been through the same.
