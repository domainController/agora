Legal Notice & Data Policy

This site collects limited personal data solely to enable early access to the OB1 forum and to foster community support.

By submitting your information, you consent to be contacted in the context of shared legal issues or workplace experiences. You may receive updates or be notified if other individuals report similar conditions or mention the same employer.

We will never share your data with third parties or employers without your explicit consent. The platform is not intended for denunciation but for empowerment, solidarity, and informed legal and administrative guidance.

You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at legal@ob1.se.

The OB1 initiative stems from personal experiences within the hospitality industry and aims to connect individuals who work under inconvenient conditions ("OB" or "Obekväm arbetstid") to collectively discuss legal avenues and shared strategies.

All submissions are stored securely. This site complies with GDPR principles and relevant Swedish labor legislation.
