What is an OB1 circle?

An OB1 Circle is a Sweden-based peer group for people working off-hours who face legal irregularities or uncertain conditions. There are three types of circles:

  • Forum Circles – A hosted forum on OB1, structured by topic and coming soon.
  • Message Circles – Planned WhatsApp or Telegram groups for real-time peer exchange.
  • Social Circles – Facebook groups or similar are considered for broader, informal sharing.
OB1 circles may be mobile-first, desktop-based, or community-managed. They grow only if enough people show interest.

OB1 Circles are participative and experimental spaces. Information shared in this form is treated with care and used only to explore community needs. No legal service is provided through this form. Responses are not binding and do not constitute formal representation or advice.
